The Faculty of Information Technology offers two programs: the Bachelor of Applied Information Technology and the Higher Diploma in Information Technology. In Faculty of Engineering we provide Higher Diploma in Quantity Surveyor for a duration of 2 years. The field relates to managing and controlling the costs and contracts of building and infrastructure projects.
