Course we offer

we offers three Faculties with Degree's

Faculty of Management

The Faculty of Management offers a full-time, two-year ACCA program, designed to provide students with a strong academic foundation for a successful career in Accounting and Finance.

Faculty of IT

The Faculty of Information Technology offers two programs: the Bachelor of Applied Information Technology and the Higher Diploma in Information Technology. The field of IT focuses on storing, processing, securing, and managing information. These programs emphasize key areas such as software development, databases, and networking.

Faculty of Engineering

In Faculty of Engineering we provide Higher Diploma in Quantity Surveyor for a duration of 2 years. The field relates to managing and controlling the costs and contracts of building and infrastructure projects.

About Us

The founding vision of ICST is rooted in the belief that higher education should be a transformative force capable of breaking down socio-economic barriers. ICST was established with the explicit purpose of providing a job oriented higher education for those with limited financial means, creating an environment where every aspiring student, regardless of their economic background, can access quality education and embark on a journey of enfranchisement. The founder envisioned a university with inclusivity that ensure students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds have equal opportunities to pursue higher education.
